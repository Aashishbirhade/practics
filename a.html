DEGARELIX FERRING

Adapted from European Association of Urology Guidelines, 2022.

DEGARELIX FERRING is indicated for the treatment of adult male patients with advanced hormone-dependent prostate cancer, also in combination with radiotherapy and as neo-adjuvant treatment prior to radiotherapy in patients with high-risk localised or locally hormone-dependent dependent prostate cancer.12,13
